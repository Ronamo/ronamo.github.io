Hi! I'm Jaron.

Hi, I'm Jaron, an Atlanta based web designer with a passion for details and a love of clean code. My educational background is in Science, Technology, & Culture (BSc in STaC, Georgia Tech), and I've been fascinated with the web ever since Netscape Navigator was the it browser.

My work focuses on typography, texture, and clarity. Semantics and emerging browser technologies are very important to me. If a site is attractive, but still doesn't convey information well both to the audience and the web at large, then it is only 50% effective.

I am a strong proponent of open source design software, as I believe that entry into the field should not be gated by the availability of the tools necessary to do the job.
